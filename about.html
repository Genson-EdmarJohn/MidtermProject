playmakers

A music organization in USTP that gathers aspiring musicians to showcase their God-given talents with great value and purpose.

Playmakers is an organization which aims to showcase God-given talents as a tool for life and community transformation.

mission

Gather skilled USTP musicians and train them to use their talents with great purpose and with values. Offers an avenue for USTP musicians to showcase their God-given talents in music. Helps USTP musicians enhance their social skills through music.

vision

The most outstanding University-recognized music organization providing quality skilled, educated and disciplined young musician that gives contribution to the society and the music industry.
